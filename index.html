    About Us


    Dear STOX Members and Participants Cordial greetings from Jammu, the city of temple on the Tawi river. We welcome you all to the International Conference on 'Toxicology in Drug Discovery: Challenges and Future Prospects' and 42nd Annual Meeting of Society of Toxicology (STOX), India being organized at CSIR-Indian Institute of Integrative Medicine, Jammu during 10-12th November, 2022. The scientific programme will comprise of invited talks by national and international distinguished toxicologists, as well as oral and poster presentations by young scientists and researchers. It will be a big summit for young researchers, students, toxicologists, pharmacologists, pathologists, environmental and occupational health scientists from academia, industry, government and regulatory agencies in India and abroad. This conference will provide a suitable platform for all professionals, researchers, and students to interact with renowned scientists of national and international reputation in the field of toxicology, exchange scientific developments, and identify emerging and frontier areas of toxicology research in India and globally.


    ABOUT THE STOX, INDIA


    The Society of Toxicology, India was founded in 1979 at CSIR-Indian Institute of Toxicology Research, Lucknow, to disseminate scientific ideas, information, and knowledge among scientists, practitioners, professionals, and policymakers about research and development in the areas of environment and occupational health or related areas of toxicology. STOX is represented by a large group of distinguished Toxicologists, Chemists, Medicos, Pharmacologists, Entomologists, Zoologists, Veterinarians, Environmentalists, and other scientists and academicians. The Society has been a founder member of the International Union of Toxicology (IUTOX) since 1980.


    ABOUT CSIR-IIIM



    CSIR-Indian Institute of Integrative Medicine (CSIR-IIIM), previously known as Regional Research Laboratory (RRL), Jammu located in the heart of Jammu city. The vision of CSIR-IIIM is to be an international center of excellence in drug discovery for natural products chemistry, chemical biology, pharmacology, and biotechnology to discover new chemical entities (NCEs) as drugs for unmet medical needs and provide scientific rationale and validity to various Indian systems of medicine. CSIR-IIIM works on all facets of natural products research and technology, preclinical and clinical validation and establishment of a mechanism of action of drugs used in various Indian systems of Medicines, commercial cultivation of high-value medicinal and aromatic plants from Western Himalayas including Kashmir Valley and Ladakh for national and international markets and to work with Indian and global pharmaceutical industry to out-license new products and technologies.


    ABOUT JAMMU



    Jammu serves as winter capital of Union Territory of Jammu and Kashmir. The city lies on uneven ridges between Shivalik hills and the Trikuta range and spreads around the Tawi river and also blessed with coniferous forests and gleaming streams. Jammu is appropriately called the City of Temples because of the profusion of shrines dotting the landscape and a travel destination on its own right. Vaishno Devi Temple is a famous holy shrine located 42 km from the city of Jammu. Bahu Fort, Mubarak Mandi Palace, Amar Mahal Palace, Mansar Lake, Patnitop, Sanasar, and Chenab rail bridge are the famous tourist spots in Jammu (www.incredibleindia.org/Jammu). Jammu is well connected by road, rail, and air from nearby cities and through New Delhi.

    Call for Abstracts



    Abstracts are invited for oral and poster presentation at the international conference of STOX 2022. The selected abstracts will be published in the conference proceedings.

    Abstract Guidelines: Abstract should not exceed 500 words including keywords, Times New Roman Font and size 12. Title of the abstract followed by Authors (indicate corresponding author with asterisk * and provide the email ID) and affiliations (Different affiliations should be numbered).


    Oral presentation


    10 minutes (Maximum)


    Poster presentation


    Size of the poster should not exceed 120 cm (height) and 90 cm (width). Content of the poster should be readable from 1.5 m distance.


    Awards for presentations at STOX 2022


    Young Scientist Award (Oral Presentation)

    There will be a young scientist awards for scientists who participant in the conference and present their salient research outcomes. The eligibility for the award shall be any young researchers below the age of 40 years as on 20.10.2022.

    Best Oral and Poster Presentation

    There will be an oral presentation and poster presentation session for research scholars/students on topics related to pharmacology and toxicology and theme of the conference. Best three oral and poster presentations will be awarded with certificates and cash prices of ₹ 4000 (First Prize), ₹ 3000 (Second Prize), and ₹ 2000 (Third Prize). Please submit the abstract via email: stoxindia2022@gmail.com. The abstracts will be reviewed by scientific committee for selection of oral and poster presentation.

    Deadline for abstract submission: 20.10.2022.
